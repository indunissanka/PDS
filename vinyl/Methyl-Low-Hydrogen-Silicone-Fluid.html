Methyl Low Hydrogen Silicone Fluid

Product Overview

Methyl Low Hydrogen Silicone Fluid is a specialized silicone fluid characterized by its low hydrogen content and methyl functionality. This fluid is widely used in various applications, including coatings, release agents, and as a reactive intermediate in silicone synthesis.

Key Features

  • Low Hydrogen Content: Provides controlled reactivity and minimizes unwanted side reactions.
  • Excellent Thermal Stability: Maintains its properties over a wide temperature range.
  • Good Compatibility: Compatible with various organic and silicone systems.
  • Low Viscosity: Facilitates easy handling and application.

Applications

1. Release Coatings

Used as a release coating for paper, films, and other substrates, providing excellent release properties and preventing adhesion.

2. Water Repellent Treatments

Employed as a water repellent treatment for textiles, leather, and masonry surfaces, imparting durable water repellency.

3. Reactive Intermediate

Serves as a reactive intermediate in the synthesis of various silicone polymers, resins, and elastomers.

4. Additives for Coatings

Added to coatings to improve mar resistance, slip, and leveling properties.

Benefits

  • Improved Release Properties: Enhances the release characteristics of coated surfaces.
  • Durable Water Repellency: Provides long-lasting water protection.
  • Versatile Reactivity: Allows for the synthesis of a wide range of silicone materials.

Safety and Handling

Please refer to the Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) for detailed safety instructions and handling precautions. Use appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) when handling this product.
