VMQ - Vinyl Silicone Resin | Tai County Chemical

Description

VMQ is a high-performance vinyl silicone resin developed through co-hydrolytic condensation. It features a three-dimensional spherical structure, ensuring superior thermal stability, excellent adhesion, and durability. This product is free from toluene, making it a safer option for various applications.

Characteristics

  • Outstanding heat resistance (-60°C to +300°C)
  • Superior weather and aging resistance
  • Water repellency and chemical stability
  • High adhesion and excellent insulating properties

Properties

  • Appearance: Translucent liquid
  • Active ingredient: 100.0%
  • Ionic type: Nonionic

Applications

  • Used in PU release agents for enhanced performance
  • Ideal for optical lens coatings, improving scratch resistance without compromising transparency
  • Commonly applied in encapsulating and protecting electronic components
  • Enhances coatings and adhesives, boosting abrasion and chemical resistance

Storage & Packaging

Store in original packaging at a controlled temperature of 5°C - 40°C for optimal shelf life (up to 8 months). Packaged in 120kg HDPE blue drums to ensure safe transportation and storage.

Processing

VMQ vinyl silicone resin can be used as-is or diluted with IPA (isopropanol) or methanol-based solvents as required by specific applications.
