VMQ - Vinyl Silicone Resin | Tai County Chemical

Description

VMQ is a high-performance vinyl silicone resin developed through co-hydrolytic condensation. It features a three-dimensional spherical structure, ensuring superior thermal stability, excellent adhesion, and durability. This product is free from toluene, making it a safer option for various applications.
